E-Click System, Game Jam.
I participated in my first 3-day game jam with the general theme Spring. At the start of the event, the specific theme Butterfly was announced.
To create a presentable game within such a short timeframe, I designed the E-Click System, inspired by the balance of a simple natural ecosystem with four core elements.
The gameplay incorporated the butterfly effect: players could “click” on elements to generate various outcomes.
However, without understanding the hidden natural rules I programmed in C++ and C#, the system could eventually collapse... unless the player discovered how to maintain balance.
In just three days, I developed the gameplay system, coded the mechanics, composed the music, and created the artwork. The game ranked #50 among all submissions.
